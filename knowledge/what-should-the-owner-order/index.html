What should the Bauherr order?

Not “a BIM model”. The owner should order the information required to make, verify and operate specific decisions.

Start from outcomes

The owner first identifies the business or operational result: model coordination, cost control, asset handover, maintenance planning, space management or portfolio reporting.

Business outcome→Use case→Information need→Format and rule→Accepted delivery

Each information order needs context

  • Purpose and related decision.
  • Responsible producer and reviewer.
  • Required object, property, identifier and classification.
  • Delivery milestone, source and format.
  • Validation rule and evidence of acceptance.
Example: BIM2FM handover is not “deliver asset data”. It is a defined set of assets and attributes, linked to FM processes, systems, identifiers and quality checks.

The benefit

This approach reduces unnecessary data, improves tender clarity and gives the project team a traceable reason for every information requirement.
